  • The cosmological simulation code CONCEPT 1.0

    • This is the primary paper on CONCEPT itself. Please cite this if you make use of CONCEPT for a publication.

    • Introduced short-range dynamics via proper P¬≥M gravity and adaptive time-stepping, as well as a vast number of other improvements (available as of release 1.0.0).

  • Fully relativistic treatment of decaying cold dark matter in N-body simulations

    • Introduced decaying cold dark matter into CONCEPT (available as of release 0.3.0).

    • arXiv: 1904.11773 \(\quad\) DOI: 10.1088/1475-7516/2019/06/032

  • Dark energy perturbations in N-body simulations

    • Introduced dark energy perturbation into CONCEPT (available as of release 0.3.0).

    • arXiv: 1904.05210 \(\quad\) DOI: 10.1088/1475-7516/2019/08/013

  • Fully relativistic treatment of light neutrinos in N-body simulations

    • Introduced linear massive neutrino, photon and metric perturbations into CONCEPT (available as of release 0.2.0).

    • arXiv: 1811.00904 \(\quad\) DOI: 10.1088/1475-7516/2019/03/022

  • \(\boldsymbol{\nu}\)CONCEPT: Cosmological neutrino simulations from the non-linear Boltzmann hierarchy

    • Introduced non-linear massive neutrinos into CONCEPT (available as of release 0.1.0).

    • arXiv: 1712.03944 \(\quad\) DOI: 10.1088/1475-7516/2019/02/052
